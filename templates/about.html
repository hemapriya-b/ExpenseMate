Simplify Your Finances

Our Mission

At Expensio, we believe managing finances should be simple, transparent, and stress-free. Whether you're planning a trip with friends, managing household expenses, or tracking personal spending, our platform provides the tools you need to stay in control of your money.

Why Choose Expensio?

Group Expense Splitting

Create rooms for events and automatically split expenses equally among members. No more manual calculations or confusion about who owes what.

Visual Insights

Get beautiful charts and graphs that show your spending patterns. Understand where your money goes with intuitive visualizations.

Export & Reports

Download detailed expense reports as CSV files. Perfect for record keeping, tax purposes, or financial analysis.

Personal Budgeting

Set monthly budgets and track your personal expenses. Get alerts and insights to help you stay within your financial goals.

Responsive Design

Access Expensio from any device – desktop, tablet, or mobile. Our responsive design ensures a seamless experience everywhere.

Secure & Private

Your financial data is safe with us. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information and ensure privacy.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Sign up for free and create your profile. It takes less than a minute to get started with Expensio.

2

Create or Join a Room

Create a room for your event or join an existing room using a unique room code shared by friends.

3

Add Expenses

Start adding expenses to your room. Include descriptions, amounts, categories, and upload receipts if needed.

4

Split Automatically

Expensio automatically calculates how much each person owes or should receive, eliminating manual calculations.

5

Track & Analyze

View charts, export reports, and gain insights into your spending patterns. Stay on top of your finances effortlessly.
